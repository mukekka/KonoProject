    Introducing Snapdragon 8 Gen 3

    Oct 24, 2023 | 1:42

    Generative AI Excellence

    Our leading AI Engine is the world’s first to support generative AI models with up to 10 billion parameters — on-device. The Qualcomm® Hexagon™ NPU  delivers 98% faster performance1 and 40% improved performance-per-watt for sustained AI inferencing.

    Premium-Level Performance

    The Qualcomm® Kryo™ CPU reaches unprecedented speeds with upgraded CPU subsystem and a 30% performance improvement and 20% improved power efficiency. Plus, the upgraded Qualcomm® Adreno™ GPU yields 25% faster graphics rendering and 25% improved GPU power efficiency, enhanced gaming and streaming.

    Intelligent Capture

    The Qualcomm Spectra™, our leading Cognitive ISP, produces AI-enhanced photos and videos in real-time for exquisite quality in any environment. Capture extraordinary content with generative AI voice-activated photo and video editing.

    Console-Defying Gaming Features

    Our full Snapdragon Elite Gaming™ arsenal is bolstered by an astonishing 240Hz display for 240 FPS ultra-smooth, lag-free experiences. Plus, experience even better authentic immersion and lifelike lighting with 40% better Hardware-Accelerated Ray  Tracing with new Global Illumination, powered by our first-in-mobile support for the Unreal Engine 5.2.

    Studio-Quality Audio, Everywhere

    Snapdragon Sound™ Technology Suite with new Qualcomm® Expanded Personal Area Network Technology (XPAN) is the world’s only solution for uninterrupted lossless audio over Wi-Fi, even when moving from room to room. Plus, conquer gaming adversaries on-the-go with ultra-low 48ms latency sound.

    Limitless Connectivity

    The Snapdragon X75 Modem-RF System offers best-in-class 5G, packing 2.5x AI processing power at the hardware level for faster, power-efficient operation, and better location accuracy. The Qualcomm® FastConnect™ 7800 Connectivity System is the only solution offering Wi-Fi 7 with High Band Simultaneous Multi-Link and Bluetooth® technologies for unstoppable speeds and reliability. 

    1All comparisons are made to previous generation 

    Features

    • Our Qualcomm® AI Engine includes:
      • Qualcomm® Hexagon™ NPU
        • 98% Hexagon NPU performance improvement 
      • Make use of virtual assistants, GPT chatbots, stable diffusion, LLM, and more with upgraded Micro Tile Inferencing —all performed on-device for ultimate security 
      • Qualcomm® Sensing Hub
        • Always-on and ultra-low power for: audio, sensors, and two always -sensing cameras
      • Support for all precisions (INT4, INT8, INT16, FP16)
    • Qualcomm® Kryo™ CPU features up to 30% improved CPU performance and 20% more power efficiency
      • The upgraded CPU subsystem has converted an additional efficiency core into a performance core for a more robust overall CPU configuration of 1 prime, 5 performance, and 2 efficiency cores. 
    • Qualcomm® Adreno™ GPU delivers up to 25% improved performance and 25% better power efficiency
    • Up to 10% overall SoC power savings 
      • Our Qualcomm Spectra™ Cognitive ISP features:
        • Real-time Semantic Segmentation for photo/video frame now with up to 12 layers
        • Dual Always-Sensing Cameras featuring AI for quick and easy face detection and increased privacy
        • Generative AI powered voice activated photo and video editing 
        • Night Vision video capture powered by AI
        • HDR photo technology from Dolby for remarkable color range and resolution
        • Video Object Eraser by Arcsoft removes unwanted objects or people from videos
        • Photo expansion intuitively extends a photo beyond what was captured 
        • Truepic photo capture with C2PA standard support lets you snap a photo with Truepic’s cryptographic seal to prove the photo is real and not Generative AI
        • Vlogger’s View lets you share both your selfie camera and rear camera simultaneously – putting you in the action as you share and narrate
      • AV1 codec for video playback
    • Full suite of Snapdragon Elite Gaming™ Features
      • The industry standard for Real-time Hardware Accelerated Ray Tracing for improved lighting, shadows – now with Global Illumination and 1.5x better Ray Tracing 
      • Snapdragon Game Super Resolution upscales gaming scenes up to 8K
      • Support for Unreal Engine 5.2 MetaHumans framework for realistic human characters
    • Featuring our integrated Snapdragon® X75 5G Modem-RF System
      • Blazing download speeds up to 10 Gigabit per second for effortless entertainment and constant productivity 
      • AI hardware acceleration further enhances your device’s 5G performance
      • 5G Dual-SIM Dual-Active (DSDA) enables the simultaneous use of two 5G+5G or 5G+4G SIM cards for ultimate user flexibility
    • Snapdragon Location Suite
      • Triple frequency support (L1/L5/L2) for improved location accuracy/positioning user experience even for consumer devices with lower quality GNSS antenna
